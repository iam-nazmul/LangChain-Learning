# Getting a Job at Anthropic

1. **Check their careers page**
   - anthropic.com/careers is the official source
   - They list open positions, requirements, and application process


2. **Typical roles available**
   - Research scientists and engineers
   - ML/AI engineers
   - Safety researchers
   - Policy and operations roles
   - Business and other functions

## Build Relevant Skills

- **For technical roles**: Strong ML/AI fundamentals, research experience, coding ability
- **For research**: Publications, deep knowledge in relevant areas
- **For non-technical**: Relevant industry experience

## Application Strategy

1. **Tailor your resume/CV** to the specific role
2. **Highlight relevant experience**: past projects, publications, achievements
3. **Apply directly** through their careers portal
4. **Consider networking**: conferences, through mutual connections, or other legitimate channels

## Competitive Factors

- Advanced degree (MS/PhD common for research roles)
- Demonstrated expertise in AI/ML
- Relevant publications or projects
- Problem-solving ability (shown in interviews/assessments)

## Reality Check

Anthropic is highly competitive. They typically look for candidates with:
- Strong technical credentials
- Research or engineering experience at top companies
- Clear expertise in their focus areas

**Best approach**: Start by visiting their careers page, understanding what they're hiring for, and assessing whether your background aligns with their needs.
