# How to Get a Job at Anthropic

## Application Process
1. Visit **anthropic.com/careers**
2. Browse open positions
3. Submit resume/CV + cover letter
4. Technical interviews (coding, ML theory, system design)
5. Culture fit interviews
6. Offer stage

---

## 10 Typical ML/AI Engineer Roles at Anthropic

### 1. **Research Scientist - LLM Alignment**
**Description:** Develop techniques to make large language models safer and more aligned with human values.
**Skills Required:**
- Deep learning & transformers
- Research publication experience
- Python, PyTorch
- Understanding of alignment/interpretability
- PhD or equivalent research experience

### 2. **ML Infrastructure Engineer**
**Description:** Build scalable systems for training and serving large language models.
**Skills Required:**
- Distributed systems
- Kubernetes, Docker
- Python/C++
- GPU/TPU optimization
- MLOps & DevOps experience

### 3. **Applied ML Engineer - Safety**
**Description:** Implement safety features and red-teaming frameworks for Claude.
**Skills Required:**
- Prompt engineering
- NLP fundamentals
- Evaluation frameworks
- Python
- Domain expertise in bias/fairness/toxicity

### 4. **Interpretability Researcher**
**Description:** Investigate how language models work internally through mechanistic interpretability.
**Skills Required:**
- Linear algebra & calculus
- PyTorch/JAX
- Research methodology
- Understanding of neural networks
- Strong mathematical foundation

### 5. **Software Engineer - API & Platform**
**Description:** Develop APIs and platforms for Claude's deployment and user interaction.
**Skills Required:**
- Backend development (Python/Go/Rust)
- API design & REST principles
- Database design (SQL/NoSQL)
- Scalability & performance optimization
- System design

### 6. **Machine Learning Engineer - Training**
**Description:** Optimize the training pipeline and develop novel training techniques for LLMs.
**Skills Required:**
- Large-scale distributed training
- CUDA/GPU programming
- PyTorch/JAX expertise
- Understanding of optimization algorithms
- Experience with transformers

### 7. **Evaluation & Benchmarking Engineer**
**Description:** Create comprehensive evaluation frameworks and benchmarks for model capabilities.
**Skills Required:**
- Metric design
- Statistical analysis
- Python/SQL
- Domain knowledge across multiple tasks
- Attention to detail for evaluation rigor

### 8. **Policy & Governance Researcher**
**Description:** Research responsible deployment practices and contribute to AI safety policy.
**Skills Required:**
- Policy research background
- Understanding of AI risks
- Writing & communication
- Domain expertise (security, ethics, law)
- Cross-disciplinary thinking

### 9. **Dialogue & Generation Researcher**
**Description:** Improve Claude's conversation quality and reasoning capabilities.
**Skills Required:**
- NLP & linguistics knowledge
- Fine-tuning techniques
- RLHF experience
- Python
- Understanding of dialogue systems

### 10. **DevOps/Infrastructure Engineer**
**Description:** Manage infrastructure, monitoring, and deployment systems at scale.
**Skills Required:**
- Linux systems administration
- Cloud platforms (AWS/GCP)
- Infrastructure as Code (Terraform)
- Monitoring & logging tools
- Networking fundamentals

## General Tips for Success

✅ **Competitive Advantages:**
- Published research or open-source contributions
- Experience with LLMs (fine-tuning, deployment)
- Demonstrated interest in AI safety
- Strong fundamentals in ML theory
- Attention to detail in your application

❌ **Common Rejections:**
- Unclear motivation for joining
- Lack of relevant technical depth
- Poor communication skills
- No concrete project examples

**Best Time:** Anthropic regularly updates positions, so check frequently and apply for multiple relevant roles if qualified.
